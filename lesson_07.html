Place Value & Basic Math

Sebelum belajar penjumlahan bersimpan, kita harus menguasai nilai tempat (Place Value). Dalam bilangan 3-digit, ada ratusan (Hundreds), puluhan (Tens), dan satuan (Ones).

Before learning regrouping addition, we must master place value. In a 3-digit number, there are Hundreds, Tens, and Ones.

Contoh: Pada angka $523$, angka $5$ berada di ratusan sehingga bernilai $500$. Angka $2$ di puluhan bernilai $20$. Angka $3$ di satuan bernilai $3$.

Example: In $523$, $5$ is in the hundreds place so it's worth $500$. $2$ is in the tens place worth $20$. $3$ is in the ones place worth $3$.

1. What is the value of the digit 5 in 523? 🤔

1. Berapakah nilai dari angka 5 pada bilangan 523?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Identify the place value of the digit 5 in 523.
  2. It is in the third position from the right, which is Hundreds.
  3. Therefore, its value is $5 \times 100 = 500$.

2. If I have 3 hundreds, 0 tens, and 7 ones, what number is that? ✌️

2. Jika aku punya 3 ratusan, 0 puluhan, dan 7 satuan, apa angka itu?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Combine the values: Hundreds + Tens + Ones.
  2. $300 + 0 + 7$.
  3. The number is 307.

3. Quickly add: $8 + 7 = $ ? 🏎️

3. Hitung cepat: $8 + 7 = $ ?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Start from 8, count up 7 more.
  2. $8 + 2 = 10$.
  3. $10 + 5 = 15$.

4. What is the missing number in this pattern: 12, 14, 16, __? 💡

4. Berapa angka yang hilang dalam pola ini: 12, 14, 16, __?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Look at the difference between numbers: $14 - 12 = 2$.
  2. The pattern is adding 2.
  3. $16 + 2 = 18$.

5. In 812, which digit is in the tens place? 🤨

5. Pada angka 812, digit manakah yang berada di tempat puluhan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Recall place values from right to left: Ones, Tens, Hundreds.
  2. In 812: 2 is Ones, 1 is Tens, 8 is Hundreds.
  3. The tens digit is 1.

6. Write the number for: 9 hundreds, 0 tens, 4 ones. ✍️

6. Tuliskan angka untuk: 9 ratusan, 0 puluhan, 4 satuan.

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Hundreds = 9, Tens = 0, Ones = 4.
  2. Combine them: $900 + 0 + 4$.
  3. The number is 904.

7. Which is larger: 321 or 312? 🧐

7. Mana yang lebih besar: 321 atau 312?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Compare the hundreds digit: both are 3.
  2. Compare the tens digit: 2 in 321 vs 1 in 312.
  3. Since 2 is greater than 1, 321 is larger.

8. What is $9 + 6$? 🎯

8. Berapakah $9 + 6$?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Make a 10 strategy: $9 + 1 = 10$.
  2. The 6 becomes 5 because we used 1.
  3. $10 + 5 = 15$.

9. Hitunglah: $18 - 5 = $ ...

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Start at 18.
  2. Count down 5 steps: 17, 16, 15, 14, 13.
  3. The answer is 13.

Real-World Connection

Bayangkan kamu seorang kolektor kelereng (marbles). Kamu punya dua kantong kelereng. Untuk mengetahui total kelerengmu, kamu harus menjumlahkan dua bilangan 3-digit.

Imagine you are a marble collector. You have two bags of marbles. To know your total marbles, you must add two 3-digit numbers.

Misalnya, kantong pertama berisi $128$ kelereng, kantong kedua berisi $115$ kelereng. Kadang jumlah satuan melebihi $9$, sehingga kita butuh trik khusus yang disebut Regrouping (menyimpan).

For example, the first bag has $128$ marbles, the second bag has $115$ marbles. Sometimes the sum of ones exceeds $9$, so we need a special trick called Regrouping.

1. You have 128 marbles in bag A and 115 marbles in bag B. What math operation do you use to find the total? 📦

1. Kamu punya 128 kelereng di kantong A dan 115 kelereng di kantong B. Operasi hitung apa yang dipakai?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. "Total" means putting things together.
  2. The operation to put numbers together is Addition.

2. A farmer has 547 grains of corn and 228 grains of rice. What is the problem asking? 🎯

2. Seorang petani punya 547 butir jagung dan 228 butir beras. Apa yang ditanyakan dari soal ini?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Look for keywords like "and" and "altogether" or "total".
  2. These keywords mean we need to find the sum of both items.

3. "When we have too many ones, they transform into a ten!" What is this magic trick called? 🤨

3. "Saat kita punya terlalu banyak satuan, mereka berubah menjadi puluhan!" Apa nama trik ajaib ini?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Trading 10 ones for 1 ten is called regrouping.
  2. In Indonesian, it's also known as "menyimpan".

4. If you add $128 + 115$, which digits do you add FIRST? 🧩

4. Jika kamu menjumlahkan $128 + 115$, digit mana yang kamu jumlahkan PERTAMA?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. In addition algorithms, we always start from the rightmost side.
  2. The rightmost digits are the Ones.

5. In $8 + 5$, the result is 13. Why is 13 "too big" for the ones place? ✍️

5. Pada $8 + 5$, hasilnya 13. Mengapa 13 "terlalu besar" untuk tempat satuan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Each place value (ones, tens, hundreds) can only hold a single digit.
  2. The digits are 0 through 9. 13 has two digits, so it doesn't fit.

6. Identify the ones digits in the problem: $246 + 137$. 🏗️

6. Identifikasi angka satuan pada soal: $246 + 137$.

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Look at the rightmost digit of each number.
  2. In 246, the ones digit is 6.
  3. In 137, the ones digit is 7.

7. You have 385 stickers and buy 107 more. What is the vertical addition format? 🤔

7. Kamu punya 385 stiker dan membeli 107 lagi. Bagaimana format penjumlahan vertikalnya?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. To add vertically, numbers are stacked aligning their place values.
  2. Hundreds under hundreds, tens under tens, ones under ones.
  3. The correct format is stacking 385 and 107 with a + sign.

8. What does "too many ones" mean in addition? 🏎️

8. Apa arti dari "terlalu banyak satuan" dalam penjumlahan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Ones place only holds values 0 to 9.
  2. If the sum is 10 or more (greater than 9), we have "too many ones".

9. Match the problem with its correct first step! 🖥️

9. Cocokkan soal dengan langkah pertamanya!

  1. Always add ones first.

10. "When ones add up to 10 or more, they move to the tens place." True or False? 🎯

10. "Ketika satuan dijumlahkan menjadi 10 atau lebih, mereka pindah ke tempat puluhan." Benar atau Salah?

Base-10 Blocks Review

Kita akan mengingat kembali 'Base-10 Blocks'. Ingat, 10 kubus satuan kecil (ones) memiliki ukuran yang persis sama dengan 1 batang puluhan (tens rod)! Saat jumlah satuan mencapai 10, kita bisa menukarnya menjadi 1 puluhan.

Let's recall 'Base-10 Blocks'. Remember, 10 small unit cubes (ones) are the exact same size as 1 tens rod! When the number of ones reaches 10, we can trade them for 1 ten.

Proses menukar 10 satuan menjadi 1 puluhan inilah yang disebut Regrouping atau menyimpan.

This process of trading 10 ones for 1 ten is called Regrouping.

1. How many small unit cubes (ones) make up 1 tens rod? 🧩

1. Berapa banyak kubus satuan kecil yang membentuk 1 batang puluhan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. A tens rod represents 10.
  2. Therefore, it takes exactly 10 unit cubes to make 1 tens rod.

2. If you have 14 ones, how many tens can you trade them for, and how many ones are left? 🛠️

2. Jika kamu punya 14 satuan, berapa banyak puluhan yang bisa ditukar, dan berapa satuan yang tersisa?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Find how many tens are in 14.
  2. $14 = 10 + 4$.
  3. You can trade 10 ones for 1 ten, leaving 4 ones.

3. Match the base-10 block amounts with their standard numbers! 🧠

3. Cocokkan jumlah blok base-10 dengan angka standarnya!

3 tens, 2 ones =
2 tens, 3 ones =
5 tens, 0 ones =
💡 See Solution Steps
  1. 3 tens ($30$) + 2 ones ($2$) = $32$.
  2. 2 tens ($20$) + 3 ones ($3$) = $23$.
  3. 5 tens ($50$) + 0 ones ($0$) = $50$.

4. If you have 2 tens rods and 12 unit cubes, what is the standard form of this number? 🤔

4. Jika kamu punya 2 batang puluhan dan 12 kubus satuan, berapakah bentuk standar angka ini?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. You have 2 tens ($20$) and 12 ones ($12$).
  2. Regroup the 12 ones: $12 = 10 + 2$.
  3. The 10 ones become 1 more ten. So you have $3$ tens total ($20 + 10$).
  4. Leftover ones is 2. So, $30 + 2 = 32$.

5. What do we call the process of trading 10 ones for 1 ten? 🏆

5. Apa yang kita sebut proses menukar 10 satuan menjadi 1 puluhan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Trading 10 of a smaller unit for 1 of a larger unit is called regrouping.

6. 1 tens rod + 5 unit cubes = ? ✍️

6. 1 batang puluhan + 5 kubus satuan = ?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. 1 ten = 10.
  2. 5 ones = 5.
  3. $10 + 5 = 15$.

7. How many ones are in 2 tens? 🚫

7. Berapa banyak satuan dalam 2 puluhan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. 1 ten = 10 ones.
  2. 2 tens = $10 + 10 = 20$ ones.

8. If you have 2 tens rods and 2 unit cubes, and your friend gives you 1 rod and 8 unit cubes. How many ones do you have total before regrouping? 📝

8. Jika kamu punya 2 batang dan 2 satuan, dan temanmu memberi 1 batang dan 8 satuan. Berapa total satuan yang kamu punya sebelum disimpan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Your ones: 2 cubes. Friend's ones: 8 cubes.
  2. Total ones before any regrouping: $2 + 8 = 10$.

9. True or False: 1 tens rod is the exact same size as 10 small unit cubes put together. ✍️

9. Benar atau Salah: 1 batang puluhan ukurannya persis sama dengan 10 kubus satuan yang disatukan.

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Base-10 blocks are designed to show volume and size equivalency.
  2. 10 unit cubes snap together to form the exact shape and size of 1 tens rod.
  3. Statement is True.

10. You have 3 tens rods and 14 unit cubes. If you regroup the ones, how many tens rods will you have in total? 🎯

10. Kamu punya 3 batang puluhan dan 14 kubus satuan. Jika kamu menyimpan satuanannya, berapa banyak batang puluhan yang kamu punya secara total?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. You start with 3 tens rods.
  2. You have 14 ones. $14 = 10 + 4$.
  3. The 10 ones become 1 new tens rod.
  4. Total rods = $3 + 1 = 4$.

Problem Introduction: $128 + 115$

Sekarang, mari kita selesaikan masalah $128 + 115$. Kita selalu mulai dari satuan (Ones).

Now, let's solve $128 + 115$. We always start from the Ones.

Satuan: $8 + 5 = 13$. Uh oh! 13 terlalu besar untuk tempat satuan (hanya boleh 1 digit). Jadi, kita ambil 10 satuan, ubah menjadi 1 batang puluhan baru, dan bawa (carry) ke 'Tens neighborhood'. Kita tinggalkan angka 3 di tempat satuan. Ini disebut regrouping!

Ones: $8 + 5 = 13$. Uh oh! 13 is too big for the ones place (only 1 digit allowed). So, we take 10 ones, make a new Ten Rod, and carry it over to the 'Tens neighborhood'. We leave the 3 behind. This is regrouping!

1. What is $8 + 5$ in the ones place of $128 + 115$? 🧩

1. Berapakah $8 + 5$ pada tempat satuan dari $128 + 115$?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Look at the ones digit of each number: 8 and 5.
  2. $8 + 5 = 13$.

2. The sum of the ones is 13. How many tens and ones is 13 made of? 🛠️

2. Jumlah satuan adalah 13. 13 terdiri dari berapa puluhan dan berapa satuan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Break down 13 into tens and ones.
  2. $13 = 10 + 3$.
  3. This is 1 ten and 3 ones.

3. In $128 + 115$, what digit stays in the ones place of the answer? 🧠

3. Pada $128 + 115$, angka berapa yang tinggal di tempat satuan pada jawabannya?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. From 13 (1 ten and 3 ones), we leave the ones behind.
  2. The ones digit is 3.

4. Where does the '1' from the number 13 go? 🤔

4. Ke mana perginya angka '1' dari bilangan 13?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. The '1' in 13 actually represents 1 ten.
  2. It must be carried over to the Tens place to be added with the other tens.

5. Now let's add the Tens: $2 + 1 + 1$ (carried). What is the sum? 🏆

5. Sekarang jumlahkan Puluhan: $2 + 1 + 1$ (yang disimpan). Berapakah hasilnya?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. The original tens are 2 (from 128) and 1 (from 115).
  2. Plus the 1 ten we carried over.
  3. $2 + 1 + 1 = 4$.

6. Finally, add the Hundreds: $1 + 1$. What is the sum? ✍️

6. Terakhir, jumlahkan Ratusan: $1 + 1$. Berapakah hasilnya?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. The hundreds digits are 1 (from 128) and 1 (from 115).
  2. $1 + 1 = 2$.

7. Put it all together. What is the total sum of $128 + 115$? 🚫

7. Gabungkan semuanya. Berapakah total dari $128 + 115$?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Hundreds sum = 2.
  2. Tens sum = 4.
  3. Ones sum = 3.
  4. Final answer: 243.

8. What do we call carrying the 1 to the Tens place? 📝

8. Apa yang kita sebut saat membawa 1 ke tempat puluhan?

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. Exchanging 10 ones for 1 ten and moving it to the next column is called regrouping.

9. True or False: You write both digits of 13 in the ones column. ✍️

9. Benar atau Salah: Kamu menulis kedua angka dari 13 di kolom satuan.

💡 See Solution Steps
  1. The ones column can only hold one digit (0-9).
  2. You must split the 13 and carry the ten over. The statement is False.
